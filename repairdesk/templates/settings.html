PayPal invoicing

Send customers a PayPal invoice from a repair (auto-filled from its issues, services, and shipping), with a review-and-confirm step before it goes out. Create a REST app at developer.paypal.com and paste its credentials here. Start in sandbox to test.

The Secret is stored in your local repairdesk.db on this Mac. Leaving the Secret blank keeps the saved one.

Reading customer replies

Pull customers' replies onto the matching ticket's chat. Use Check replies on the Repairs page (or a scheduled job that POSTs to /inbound/poll). Replies are matched to a customer by phone/email, and to a ticket by any repair number in the message — otherwise their most recent ticket.

Reads the Messages history (~/Library/Messages/chat.db). You must grant this app Full Disk Access in System Settings → Privacy & Security. Mac only.
For Gmail use an App Password (same kind as the SMTP one). Password is stored in your local repairdesk.db; blank keeps the saved one. Messages are marked read as they're imported.
